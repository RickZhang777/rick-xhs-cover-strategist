# Title Hooks

Use short, spoken, high-contrast titles. Main title usually 4-12 Chinese characters. Avoid long article headlines.

## 痛点型

- 别再乱选了
- 你卡在这一步
- 越整理越乱
- 先别急着买工具
- 小白最容易踩坑

Use when the audience already feels pain or confusion.

## 结果型

- 3天搭好获客页
- 一套流程跑通
- 从0做出样品
- 7步完成冷启动
- 省掉一半时间

Use when the content has a clear method, tutorial, or outcome.

## 反常识型

- 先别急着投流
- 官网不是第一步
- 便宜工具更好用
- 不是你不努力
- 越自动越低效

Use when the user wants a sharp point of view.

## 人群型

- 新手先看
- 家长一定要懂
- 工具小白别错过
- 独立开发者必备
- 运营别再手搓

Use when audience targeting is more important than novelty.

## 对比测评型

- 5款工具实测
- 7个维度横评
- 我替你试过了
- 到底值不值
- 谁最适合新手

Use for products, tools, templates, courses, or solutions.

## 信任证明型

- 我的交付清单
- 用户为什么买单
- 这套方案能复用
- 不是理论
- 实操结果公开

Use for consulting, courses, service offers, and case studies.

## Title Compression Rules

- Replace abstract words with concrete outcome words.
- Put the most emotional word first when possible.
- Use numbers only when they make the promise more credible.
- Avoid more than 2 punctuation marks.
- Keep subtitles short: 6-14 Chinese characters.
